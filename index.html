How Can I Create a Microsoft 365 Account?


You can create a Microsoft 365 account from Microsoft365.com/setup using the steps mentioned below:
  1. First, Open your web browser.
  2. Now visit microsoft365.com/setup.
  3. Here, search for “Create a new account” and hit the same.
  4. Now, you will be redirected to the next window where you need to provide some of your personal details.
  5. Enter the asked details and hit the submit button.
  6. Now, create a password and follow prompts to create a Microsoft account.

Activate Microsoft 365 Setup | Microsoft365.com/setup


To activate your microsoft365 follow these steps :
  1. Open any office application such as Word or PowerPoint.
  2. Then, press the Activate button.
  3. Enter a 25- character office product key in the space given.
  4. Log in to your microsoft365.com/setup Account for activating your MS Office.
  5. You may click on Sign up if you are not a registered user.
  6. Restart the device after following all the prompts.
